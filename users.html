Shelly McDaniel

[Back to top]


Shelly just started her senior year of high school and will be applying for colleges this Fall. She is considering the University of Louisiana, Lafayette because it is in-state, which is important in terms of both distance and cost. She has watched her three older siblings leave for college, and they all stayed close to home. She is very close with her family, and although she won't admit it to her friends, she's looking forward to bringing home her laundry and having her mom pack her copious amounts of food to bring back with her.

Shelly's grandfather taught her how to play chess when she was four years old, and she is now a nationally ranked chess player. She is extremely interested in the legal system and hopes to become a lawyer, although she is unsure exactly what type of law she wants to specialize in. She is equally ambitious in her social goals, hoping not only to meet her future bridesmaids but also begin the search for the elusive Mr. Right.

Scenario 1

Scenario Context

Shelly is comparing the pre-law programs at each of the different colleges she is interested in applying to.

Scenario Question

What kind of pre-law programs exist at the University of Louisiana?

User Task

Find out what pre-law program is offered at the University of Louisiana.

User Steps

  1. Go to Home Page
  2. Click on Student Life on primary navigation
  3. Click on Academic Programs link under "Academic Programs"
  4. Select "L" from the list of letters under "Departments by Alphabet"
  5. Click on "(See Political Science)" link next to "Law"
  6. Explore the Political Science Dept's website

Scenario 2

Scenario Context

Shelly is at her grandparents' house for Sunday dinner, and her grandfather mentions that he played in many intercollegiate chess tournaments as a college student. Shelly wonders if the University of Louisiana, Lafayette has a chess team.

Scenario Question

Is there a chess club or organization I can join?

User Task

Find a list of student organizations at UL Lafayette and search for something related to chess.

User Steps

  1. Go to Home Page
  2. Click on Student Life on primary navigation
  3. Click on Student Organization Information under "Campus Organizations"
  4. Click on Join an Organization
    5. on the main navigation of the Student Organizations website
  5. Click on Organization List –alpha order
  6. Use Command +F to search the page and type in "Chess"
  7. No result found
  8. Type in "louisiana.edu" in the browser to get back to the main site
  9. Click on Student Life on primary navigation
  10. Click Search
  11. Type in "chess club"
  12. 7 results found no chess club

Scenario 3

Scenario Context

Shelly has decided to apply to UL Lafayette, and she knows that many schools offer scholarships, in addition to all the private scholarships out there. She definitely plans to apply for scholarships and file her FAFSA, and she knows the FAFSA isn't available until after January 1st. She isn't sure what scholarships UL Lafayette offers, or when the deadlines are this year.

Scenario Question

What scholarships can Shelly apply for and how does she apply?

User Task

Find a list of scholarships and application instructions and deadlines.

User Steps

  1. Go to Home Page
  2. Click on link for Prospective Students
  3. Click on link for Freshman Students
  4. Click on link for Scholarships under "Money Matters"
  5. Click on link for High School Seniors

Dr Abdul Mohammad

[Back to top]


I have spent the last 20 years working at the University of Louisiana. Now at the young age of 90, I feel that it is time to inquire about retirement. Over the years, I have lived all over, from Saudi Arabia to Macau, but consider Louisiana my home. My children currently have their own families in New York City, and I plan to visit them after this semester ends. Some of my hobbies include teaching, reading, golf, and spending time with my dogs. I am also a fan of the arts, particularly sculpture and painting. I often visit the gallery on campus, and was thinking of volunteering my time after retirement.

Scenario 1

Scenario Context

Dr. Mohammad is planning to retire soon and wanted to find out more on organizing his exit from the University of Louisiana.

Scenario Question

What is available on the University of Louisiana website to assist Dr. Mohammad submit his request?

User Task

Find the information page that information about retirement.

User Steps

  1. Go to Home Page
  2. Click on Student Life on primary navigation
  3. Click on Faculty and Staff
  4. Under Services and Facilities, clicked on Human Resources
  5. Found a list of names and telephone numbers to assist with this process

Scenario 2

Scenario Context

Dr. Mohammad is wondering what if the Chemistry department has any additional information for retiring professors. He wants to be sure he is taken care of when he retires.

Scenario Question

What resources are available on the Chemistry webpage in the University of Louisiana?

User Task

Navigate to the chemistry page from the main home page find some employee resources.

User Steps

  1. Go to Home Page
  2. Click on Academics on primary navigation
  3. Clicked on Undergraduate and Graduate Programs: Colleges and Departments
  4. Navigated to Chemistry Department
  5. From the looks of it UL does not have a separate Chemistry department page, but rather just a page within their home site. They did not have any additional information.

Scenario 3

Scenario Context

Being the lover of arts as Dr. Mohammad mentioned, he was wondering about possibly opportunities volunteering at the arts department in the university.

Scenario Question

Are any of the university art galleries accepting volunteers? Any art museums on campus accepting volunteers?

User Task

Go on the University of Louisiana website and find out if Dr. Mohammad can apply to be a volunteer.

User Steps

  1. Go to Home Page
  2. Clicked on Student Life
  3. Clicked on Art Museum
  4. Landed on the Paul and Lulu Howard University Art Museum page
  5. Located the hidden navigation and found the Contact us page
