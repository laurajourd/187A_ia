Mary is a stay-at-home mom, although recently she began taking online classes to brush up on her accounting skills. She has served as the President of the Booster Club at her children's high school for the last three years, and she was Treasurer of the Parents' Association before that.

Having already sent three of her four children off to college, Mary knows this process can be confusing and stressful. She believes that choosing the right college is a critical first step toward adulthood, but, as any parent knows, it's always a little hard to let them go.

Her top concerns are finances, campus health and safety, and career services.

Scenario 1

Scenario context

Mary is planning a college trip for her daughter, and she knows from her previous experience that some colleges require advance reservation for campus tours. Her youngest is currently in her junior year, so the trip is scheduled for Spring break.

Scenario question

Are campus tours offered in April? If so, how can I make a reservation?

User task

Find a schedule of campus tours and make a reservation for the desired date, if necessary.

User steps

  1. Go to university site
  2. Click on link to Visitors portal:
  3. Click on "Campus Tours"
  4. Click on "Schedule Your Tour Online!"
  5. Choose date and time desired
  6. Create an account with eCampusTours.com
  7. Login to eCampusTours.com --> Advanced college search page
  8. Return to UL Lafayette tours
  9. Fill out and submit registration form

Scenario 2

Scenario context

After visiting a few colleges, her daughter has decided on 7 different schools to apply to. Mary is doing a preliminary review of the tuition and fees of each school.

Scenario question

What is the total cost for an undergraduate student, including housing and dining, for the current schoolyear, and what is the projected amount for next year?

User task

Find a breakdown of undergradaute tuition and fees for the current and, if possible, future schoolyears.

User steps

  1. Go to university site
  2. Click on link to "Prospective Students" portal
  3. Click on "Freshman Students"
  4. Click on "Estimated Costs"

Scenario 3

Scenario context

Mary's daughter has received her acceptance letters and now needs to make her final decision. Mary knows her daughter is mainly concerned about campus life, but Mary wants her daughter to attend a school that also provides ample opportunity to explore different career paths.

Scenario question

What kind of career services does the school offer, especially in regards to helping students find internship opportunities?

User task

Locate the website for the career services center, if there is one, and find information on student internships.

User steps

  1. Go to university site
  2. "Current Students" portal
  3. "Career Services" under "Other Services"
  4. "Families"
  5. "Overview of Services"
