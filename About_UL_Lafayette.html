     UL Lafayette is devoted to excellence in teaching, learning, and research, and to developing leaders in many disciplines who make a difference globally. UL Lafayette faculty are engaged with teaching and research to push the boundaries of human knowledge. The University has twelve degree-granting Schools in addition to the Radcliffe Institute for Advanced Study, offering a truly global education.

 

 

     Established in 1853, UL Lafayette is the oldest institution of higher education in the United States. The University, which is based in Lafayette, Lousiana, has an enrollment of over 20,000 degree candidates, including undergraduate, graduate, and professional students. UL Lafayette has more than 360,000 alumni around the world.
