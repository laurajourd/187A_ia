     UL Lafayette offers extraordinary scope. Students arrive with a remarkable diversity of interests as well as a wide variety of personal and professional goals or perhaps no clearly defined goals at all. What they share are keen intellectual curiosity, energy and an eagerness to fulfill their considerable promise.

 

    They seek a university with the resources to enable them to pursue their interests vigorously, whether their chosen area is philosophy, photography or physics; literature or linguistics; engineering or environmental studies. They find fellow students and teachers with whom they can share their passions and discover new ones, while forging friendships that will last a lifetime.
