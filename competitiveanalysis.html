Competitive Analysis of 5 University Websites

In order to build our redesign of the UL Lafayette website, we explored five other University websites.

The first goal was to write down the different items found on the main navigation bar of each website, and, on the second navigational levels.

Following this step we counted the number of same items found in the second level pages (Main Navigation Count).

The last step consisted to compare the main labels with the actual Louisiana's labels. Then, we chose what we will insert in our own website.


The idea is to list the navigational labels and then list the number that are in common.This is a useful place to begin our own discussion of redesign the IA and also to present to the class when we explain our design. That is, when explaining our design we can start by reviewing the main sites that gave us inspiration.
